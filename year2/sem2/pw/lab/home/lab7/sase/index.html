Simple Cooking Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the vegetable oil, milk, vanilla extract, and egg to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Grease a muffin tin and pour the batter into the cups, filling each one about 2/3 full.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before removing the muffins from the tin.
