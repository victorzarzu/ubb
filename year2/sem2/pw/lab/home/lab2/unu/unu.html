Makama signed his first professional contract with Lincoln on 17 May 2021.[3] On 11 August 2021, he joined Gainsborough Trinity on loan with Lincoln City teammate Hayden Cann for the season.[4] Following his season at Gainsborough he would commit his long-term future at Lincoln.[5] He would make his Lincoln City debut against Doncaster Rovers in the EFL Cup on 9 August 2022.
